About US:

Based in Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist. Our chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and we have a menu of 12-15 items that rotates seasonally.
